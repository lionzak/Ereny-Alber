Education website

This project showcases my redesign of an educational website with the goal of enhancing user experience, accessibility, and visual clarity. The original design was evaluated for usability, navigation flow, and engagement. Based on research and modern UI/UX principles, I restructured the interface to be more intuitive, responsive, and aligned with the needs of diverse learners. The redesign focuses on clean layouts, clear information hierarchy, and interactive elements that support a seamless learning experience.

Tools: Figma

Medical App

This project presents the redesign of a medical application with a user-centered approach aimed at improving accessibility, usability, and overall user experience. The original app, while functionally sound, lacked intuitive navigation and visual clarity, especially for users with diverse medical needs. Through detailed research, usability testing, and iterative design processes, the new interface was crafted to meet modern UX/UI standards while ensuring compliance with medical usability guidelines. This redesign reflects a deep understanding of the users' needs and aims to support better health management through clear, efficient, and empathetic design.

Tools: Figma
